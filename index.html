My name is Kevin Stowe. I'm in my third year of the Linguistics Phd program at the University of Colorado, under the direction of professor Martha Palmer. My research is primarily in the capabilities of NLP systems for social media, as well as the automatic identification and understanding of metaphors. I'm also curious about machine learning, logic, semantics, and the methods for extracting meaning from natural language accurately and automatically.
